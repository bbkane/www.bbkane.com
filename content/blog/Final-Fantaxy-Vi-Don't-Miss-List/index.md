# Part 1: The World of Balance (The First Half)

- **Narshe & South Figaro (Delayed Rewards):** **Don't open chests** in the Narshe Mines, the Cave to South Figaro, or the hidden crates/barrels in South Figaro town. They "level up" into endgame gear if you wait until the second half of the game (world of ruin). Exception: While navigating the mines during this "Moogle Defense" mini-game, look for a **small, dead-end path** leading off to the right side of the screen to get the Ribbon (not in Pixel Remaster).
- **Mt. Kolts (The Main Gauche):** Look for a hidden path through the wall on the right side of the first cave area. It’s a dagger that boosts evasion.
- **Returner’s Hideout (The Genji Glove):** **Refuse Banon three times** to get this relic.
- **Returner’s Hideout (Hidden White Cape):** Find the secret passage in the treasure room for this early-game defensive boost.
- **Imperial Camp (Dobermans & Satellite):** During Sabin’s scenario, **kick the chest** in the first tent and fight the doberman and fight the "Monster-in-a-box" in the second tent. This is the only way to get these onto Gau's Rage list.
- **The Veldt (Stray Cat Rage):** At the end of Sabin's scenario, you get Gau. Immediately look for a **Stray Cat** on the Veldt and "Leap" on it. Its **Catscratch** move (4x damage) is a game-changer for the first half of the game.
- **Zozo (The Chainsaw):** Set the clock to **6:10:50** to unlock Edgar’s best early-game tool.
- **Shadow Dreams** - stay at Inns with Shadow multiple times
- **Magitek Research Facility (Ifrit & Shiva):** **Pick up the Magicites** off the floor after the fight. If you leave the room, they are gone.
- **Magitek Research Facility (The Kazekiri):** Use Locke to **Steal** from the boss **Number 128**
- **Jidoor Auction House (Golem & Zone Seek):** Now that you've finished the Magitek Facility, return to the Auction House. These two essential Magicites should now be available for purchase.
- **Narshe Mid-Game (Saving Mog):** Once you have the airship, go to Narshe and chase Lone Wolf to the cliff. **Stand still** and wait for the stand-off to resolve, then choose to **Save Mog.**
- **The Serpent Trench (Water Rondo):** **CRITICAL:** After recruiting Mog, fly to Crescent Mountain and jump into the Serpent Trench. This is your only chance to teach Mog his **Water Rondo** dance before the world changes.
- **Cave to the Sealed Gate (Ultima Weapon):** Look for the hidden floor switch to find this iconic sword.
- **The Floating Continent (Shadow):** **CRITICAL:** At the end, choose **"Wait,"** then **"Wait for Shadow"** until the timer hits **0:05**.

# Part 2: The World of Ruin (The Second Half)

- **Solitary Island (Saving Cid):** Catch the **fast-moving fish** to keep Cid alive.
- **Duncan’s Cabin (Bum Rush):** As soon as you find Sabin (near Tzen), head to the cabin north of Narshe to learn his ultimate Blitz.
- **Triangle Island (Gogo):** Once you have the airship, fly to the top-right island and let the "Zone Eater" swallow you to find Gogo.
- **Flying in Airship** - fight Deathgaze to get Bahamut (there's also 8 dragons somewhere)
- **Narshe Mines (Mog & Umaro):** Return to Narshe to pick up Mog again. Then, take him to the back of the mines to defeat the yeti, Umaro, who will then join you.
- **Ancient Castle (Odin/Raiden):** Ride Figaro Castle through the desert until it stops.
  - *Note:* Keep **Odin** if you want the +1 Speed bonus when leveling; upgrade to **Raiden** if you prefer the +2 Strength bonus.
- **Phoenix Cave (Locke):** This is a two-party dungeon. Completing it is the only way to recruit Locke.
- **Doma Castle** - Sleep here with Cyan in party for Alexander Magicite, gear, backstory
- **Move Figaro Castle underground** - Get Odin Magicite (+1 Speed) or transform to Raiden (+2 Strength)
- **Narshe (Cursed Shield & Ragnarok):** **Requires Locke.** Now that he's back, go to the Narshe Weapon Shop. You’ll get the Cursed Shield (which becomes the **Paladin Shield** after 256 battles) and the choice of the **Ragnarok Magicite** or **Sword**.
- Bring Relm to key areas (Owzer's Mansion, etc.)
- **Coliseum**
  - Bet Ragnarok (sword) or other high tier item and win the fight to win Economizer (all spells cost 1 MP)
- **Kefka’s Tower**
  - **(Fixed Dice):** Found in a chest in the final dungeon. Essential for Setzer.
  - **(Force Field):** Have Strago alive in your party when fighting **The Fiend** to learn his final Lore.
  - **Offering / Master's Scroll** - relic chest

# Gau's Rages

To learn these, you must first encounter the monster in the world, then go to **The Veldt**, use the **"Leap"** command, and wait for Gau to return in a later battle.

Since Gau cannot use weapons, his stats come entirely from his **Armor** and **Relics**.

- **Snow Muffler:** This is Gau’s best armor (won by betting a Behemoth Suit at the Coliseum). It gives him such high defense that most physical attacks will do **0 damage** to him.
- **Merit Award:** This relic (also found at the Coliseum) allows Gau to equip weapons and armor he normally couldn't, like the **Paladin Shield**, making him a literal tank.

| Rage                       | Location                           | Key Move    | Notes                                             |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **Nightshade**             | Owzer's Mansion, Jidoor (WoR)      | Charm       | Forces enemy self-targeting; works on most bosses |
| **Aspiran**                | Serpent Trench (WoB)               | Gigavolt    | Strongest magic attack in first half              |
| **Anguiform**              | Serpent Trench (WoB)               | Aqua Breath | Multi-target Water/Wind; great mid-game           |
| **Stray Cat**              | South Figaro / Imperial Camp (WoB) | Catscratch  | 4x physical damage; relevant all game             |
| **Magic Dragon**           | Phoenix Cave (WoR)                 | Giga Flare  | Non-elemental, ignores magic defense, hits all    |
| **Behemoth King (Undead)** | Veldt Cave (WoR)                   | Holy        | Strongest single-target Holy spell                |
