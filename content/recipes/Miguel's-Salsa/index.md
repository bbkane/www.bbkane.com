# Ingredients

- 1 1/2 roasted jalepenos
- 1 bunch of Cilantro
- 5 fresh onions
- 3 cans of tomato juice
- 2 cans of tomato
- 1/2 cup salt
- 1/2 cup white vinegar
- 1/2 SS Gran garlic
- 1/2 SS Black Pepper
- 1/2 SS Oregano
- 1/2 SS Cumin

# Instructions

Blend it all together
