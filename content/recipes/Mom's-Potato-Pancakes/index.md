Make the batter (for 3 people)

- beat 3 eggs
- add a cup of milk (trial and error)
- add vegetable oil (1/4 cup)
- add 1/2 - 1tsp salt
- add 1 cup flour
- add 1/2-1tsp baking powder
- shred 2-3 potatoes
- whisk it all together

Cook

- fry in pan with hot oil and cook till brown
