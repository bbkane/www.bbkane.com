# Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 log (8oz) cream cheese
- 2 sticks (8oz) butter
- 1 tsp vanilla

# Instructions

- Mix wet ingredients
- Mix dry ingredients 
- Combine
- Using cookie press, form cookies. On our cookie have to press about 1.5 times. Then, if using the flower pattern, press down the cookies a little bit with your hand so the petals don't separate
- Bake at 375 for 15m
